Welcome to Running Routes

Enter in your location to the textbox below, or press the locate me button to allow the browser to access your location.
The ending location is optional and is the starting location by default.


Enter in a number for the distance you want to run and select a unit in the boxes below.

*Due to free API limits, generating a route may take some time. Please allow a few minutes to pass in order for a route to generate.
*These limits also mean that the route displayed on the map and the distance may not be fully accurate.
*However a Google Maps Route with the accurate path and distance will be linked, and a written path will be listed under the map.
